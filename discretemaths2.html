In this section we continue with the study of discrete mathematics. This corresponds to the second half of the “Mathematics for Computing” module run by the University of London. The exam papers are in the “Discrete Maths 1″ menu The sections are as follows
  1. Digraphs and Relations
  2. Sequences and Series
  3. Trees
  4. Counting and Probability
  5. Systems of Linear Equations and Matrices
