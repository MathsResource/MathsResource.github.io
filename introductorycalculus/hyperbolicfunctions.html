The hyperbolic cosine is the function $$\cosh x ={e^x +e^{-x }\over2},$$ and the hyperbolic sine is the function $$\sinh x ={e^x -e^{-x}\over 2}.$$

As integration and differentiation are so important in mathematics, these topics are given their own special section on this site. More advanced topics will be continued on the "Calculus 2" page
