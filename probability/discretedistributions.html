Discrete Probability Distributions

  • There are two families of Probability Distributions: the discrete and the continuous families.
  • The discrete family of distributions include the Binomial distribution, the Poisson Distribution and the Geometric distribution.
  • The continuous family of distributions include the Normal distribution, the Exponential distribution and the Continuous Uniform Distribution
  • (Remark: There are other distributions in both families, but are not considered here).
  • In addtion to studying the distributions, we will look at how some distributions can be used to approximate others, in particular the Poisson Approximation of the Binomial Distribution.
