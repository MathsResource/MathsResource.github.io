Advanced Topics for Linear Algebra

Data Analysis for Business Decisions

Syllabus:
  1. Data and Statistics – various types of data, qualitative and quantitative data, sources of data.
  2. Graphical presentation of data – bar charts, pie charts, histograms, ogive curves, box plots.
  3. Measures of location and spread – mean, median, mode, range, standard deviation and variance.
  4. Introduction to probability – discrete and continuous distributions e.g. Binomial, Poisson and Normal.
  5. Sampling and Sampling Distributions – populations and samples, various sampling methods.Point and Interval estimation for means, variances and proportions in one and two sample applications.
  6. Hypothesis testing – One and two tailed tests, type I and type II errors, p – values.
  7. Analysis of qualitative data – contingency tables, goodness – of – fit tests.
  8. Correlation and Linear Regression – scatter plots, method of least squares, use of residuals to validate model. Analysis of Variance.
  9. Multiple Regression – multicollinearity, dummy variables, model assumptions, variable selection procedures.
  10. Applications of statistics – forecasting, quality control, index numbers, decision analysis.
  11. Non- parametric Statistics – sign test Wilcoxon signed – rank, Mann – Whitney and Krusaal – Wallis tests. Spearman-s test for linear correlation.
